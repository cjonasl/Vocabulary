Commands:
f {word}: Find a word
p1 {max row length}: Prints all words comma separated
eum: Prints all examples unmasked
em: Prints all examples masked
et: Prints all examples translated
ls: Show location and size for main form and textbox
sls: Set location and size for main form and textbox, 8 blank separated integers: locationMainFormX, locationMainFormY, sizeMainFormWidth, sizeMainFormHeight, locationTextBoxX, locationTextBoxY, SizeTextBoxWidth, SizeTextBoxHeight
study: study fromWord (an integer) toWord (an integer) showWordFirst (true or false)
